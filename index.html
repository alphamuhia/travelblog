Meet Alpha Peter

Hello! My name is Alpha Peter, and I’m a web designer and student at Zindua Coding School. I have a passion for creating intuitive, user-friendly websites that blend creativity and functionality. Along with my technical skills, I have a deep love for travel, food, and exploring new cultures.

Over the years, I’ve discovered that traveling around Kenya has inspired not just my love for local cuisine but also my desire to connect with different communities and learn from their unique perspectives.

What to Expect from My Blog

In this blog, I’ll be sharing various aspects of my life and interests. Here’s a glimpse of what you can expect:

  • Web Design: I’ll showcase some of my web projects, design tips, and the journey of learning coding at Zindua Coding School.
  • Travel: I’ll take you along on my adventures across Kenya, sharing stories, travel tips, and beautiful photos of the places I visit.
  • Local Cuisine: Join me as I explore Kenya’s culinary diversity! I’ll share recipes, recommend amazing local dishes, and highlight top restaurants and hotels.
  • Hobbies: Get insights into my favorite anime, movies, music, and more! I’ll be sharing playlists, trailers, and recommendations for anyone who shares my love for entertainment.

Whether you’re here for the tech, the travel, the food, or the fun, I’m sure you’ll find something that resonates with you. Let’s explore the world together!
